Image 2k23 • Mar 21st '23

PAPER PRESENTATION

An individual or group of participants may present their topics during the paper presentation in front of the juries. Within 15 minutes, participants should cover their ece-related or emerging technology-related topics. The presentation needs to be in PPTX format.
RULES :
Per team consist of Maximum 3 members.
Background should be white/light colours Preferable
Presentation should consist of maximum 10-15 slides.

Image 2k23 • Mar 21st '23

PROJECT EXPO

Project Expo is a group event where participants can showcase their unique ideas by presenting a functioning prototype in front of judges and submit a project report.

RULES :
Participants can perform as an individual or a team.
per team consist of maximum three members.
participants should bring their working model of the project.

Image 2k23 • Mar 21st '23

DEBUGGING

CODE DEBUGGING :
Team members should identify the errors and debug the code,To verify that the outcome is proper for all valid test cases.
CIRCUIT DEBUGGING :
The competition's goal is to test participant's abilities to spot problems in a specific circuit and validate them with the desired output achieved.
RULES :
Participants should perform only as a team of two members.

Image 2k23 • Mar 21st '23

LINE FOLLOWER

A device that can recognise and follow a line drawn on the floor is a line follower robot. The path is typically predetermined and it can be a black line on a surface.
RULES :
Participants can perform as an individual or a team. per team consists of maximum three members.
