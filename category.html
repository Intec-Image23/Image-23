Symposium • Mar 21st '23

PAPER PRESENTATION

An individual or group of participants may present their topics during the paper presentation in front of the juries. Within 15 minutes, participants should cover their ece-related or emerging technology-related topics. The presentation needs to be in PPTX format.
RULES : Per team consist of 2 members. background should be white/light colours , font should be in black. presentation should consist of minimum 10 slides.

Wade Warren

Symposium • Mar 21st '23

PROJECT EXPO

Project Expo is a group event where participants can showcase their unique ideas by presenting a functioning prototype in front of judges and submitting a project report. RULES : Per team consist of 3 members.

Wade Warren

DEBUGGING

CODE DEBUGGING : Team members should identify the errors and debug the code,To verify that the outcome is proper for all valid test cases. CIRCUIT DEBUGGING : The competition's goal is to test participant's abilities to spot problems in a specific circuit and correct them such that the desired output is achieved.

LINE FOLLOWER

A device that can recognise and follow a line drawn on the floor is a line follower robot. The path is typically predetermined and can be seen as a black line on a white surface.
